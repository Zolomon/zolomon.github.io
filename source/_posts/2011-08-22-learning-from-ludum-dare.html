You have probably not heard about the indie game developer competition called Ludum Dare. It's a rapid game creation community where you compete against each other to complete a game within 48 hours and then rate the games produced during the weekend. After two weeks a winner will be named based on who got the most votes.

The difference, on the 21st occurrence of Ludum Dare, was that the creator of MineCraft, @notch (Markus Persson), decided to join the tournament as he's done a few times before. Enter Publicity.

The source code for these games are available on the website, such is the requirement of Ludum Dare - and there is much to learn from each one of them!
